Milestone 1

Purpose:

This project will be a professional portfolio website demonstrating the value I could add to any organization. It will showcase my best graphic design/multimedia projects, focusing on my skills in front-end web development. It will call out my qualifications and experience to help highlight myself as a potential candidate for any employer.

Audience:

My site is designed with a professional audience in mind; it is targeted toward individuals within the web development, multimedia, I.T., and cleared community. My target audience includes recruiters, hiring managers, start-ups founders, peers, and collaborators.

Goal:

These days, almost 15 years after it was first proposed, the clearfix is losing a bit of relevance. CSS Grid and Flexbox are filling in the gaps for advanced layout on the web. In January of 2017, Rachel Andrew wrote an article for her blog titled “The end of the clearfix hack?” In it, she describes a way to replace the clearfix hack with a single line of code using a new display mode rule known as flow-root.

Persona:

Name:

Dillon Wynot

Profession:

Dillon is a technical recruiter for a government contracting company. He finds talent with specialized skills to fulfill critical specialized roles in IT and media that provided support to their government clients and partners such as the DoD, DoJ, DIA and DoS. The need to fill these roles is especially important to maintain current contracts and sustain their reputable image in providing world-class support.

Technology:

Dillon is power user. He is well versed in all the productivity software to help maintain his schedule and stay on task. He conducts virtual meetings and interviews regularly. He generally relies on a Mac Air Pro for travel due to its long body life but has a professional PC setup at the office where he does most of his work.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

Dillon is educated and a business savvy professional, he has plenty of pressure and incentive to perform well at his Job. Finding quality reliable talent is becoming increasingly difficult and he does not have time to waste. He needs a quick a way to assess whether potential candidates have the skills and aptitude to recommend them for further consideration or hire.

Frustration:

Dillon is having a having difficult time finding enough talent to fill available government contracting roles. Many of his clients are requesting candidates with a varied number of skills so they can get the most with the money the salary they pay. Additionally, the roles needed are within agencies that handle sensitive information and need candidates with active clearances, so they fill the roles and on board as quickly as possible.

Goal:

Dillon is trying to throw a wide net and looking for talented cleared individuals on several sites to include Clearance Jobs and LinkedIn. Once he initiates contact with his pool of candidates, he needs a quick way to validate whether I should be recommended further or initiate contact.

User Story:

As a recruiter, I need a quick way to see if a candidate has the skill and qualifications to assess further and initiate contact.
