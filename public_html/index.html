About Me:

My name is Jory Richards, and I am a multimedia professional and full-stack web developer specializing in creating effective, eye-catching designs and fluid user experiences. I have been in this field for over nine years, and I have thrived in meeting the challenges of each of my client’s unique projects. I am a designer, developer, photographer, and overall a problem-solver. I am always trying to learn something new to keep up with current techniques and provide a first-class service. Comic Cover Series for Social Media

The comic book cover series was one of my favorite projects while serving as a Senior Multimedia Specialist for the U.S. Army. It highlighted the specialty platoons of “The Old Guard,” the oldest active infantry regiment of the United States Army. I enjoyed the challenge of creating a product that could garner the appeal of a younger generation and still convey the honorable service our military provides to our nation every day because there is nothing comical about that.

Manual Sketches and Vector

Do you need a resizable image that can be re-used and looks good in any medium? How about something custom? Usually, my clients already have branding, but they don't have a usable digital copy that accurately represents the brand's professionalism. More Social Media Graphics

How about social media? I have created hundreds of social media products to help promote national observances and various organizational events. Do you need it in print?
