Comic Cover Series for Social Media

The comic book cover series was one of my favorite projects while serving as a Senior Multimedia Specialist for the U.S. Army. It highlighted the specialty platoons of "The Old Guard," the oldest active infantry regiment of the United States Army. I enjoyed the challenge of creating a product that could garner the appeal of a younger generation and still convey the honorable service our military members provide to our nation every day because there is nothing comical about that.

Manual Sketches and Vector

Do you need a resizable image that can be re-used and looks good in practically any medium? How about something custom? Usually, my clients already have branding, but they don't have a useable digital copy that accurately represents the brand's professionalism. I can recreate complex images or something completely original in vector to be scaled and used as many times as it's needed.

More Social Media Graphics

How about social media? I have created hundreds of social media products to help promote national observances and various organizational events. Do you need it in print? I can also help you there— I always create my products in print resolution first and sample it down for the web. I like to think several steps ahead, and I'm prepared for when clients enjoy my creations so much they want them hanging on their walls.

Motion Graphics
