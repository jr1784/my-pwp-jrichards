About Me:

My name is Jory Richards, and I am a multimedia professional and full-stack web developer specializing in creating effective, eye-catching designs and fluid user experiences. I have been in this field for over nine years, and I have thrived in meeting the challenges of each of my client’s unique projects. I am a designer, developer, photographer, and overall a problem-solver. I am always trying to learn something new to keep up with current techniques and provide a first-class service.
